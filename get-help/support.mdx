# Support

Need help with SteerCode? We're here to help you succeed! Choose from several support options based on your needs.

## Email Support

For questions, problems, or general inquiries, reach out to our team at:

**hello@steercode.com**

We typically respond within 24 hours during business days.

## Before You Contact Support

To help us assist you faster, please include:

- **Description** of the issue or question
- **Steps** to reproduce the problem (if applicable)
- **Screenshots** or screen recordings (if helpful)
- **Browser/device** information (for technical issues)
- **SteerCode plan** you're using

## Common Support Topics

- **Account & Billing** - Plan changes, payment issues, account access
- **Technical Issues** - App crashes, performance problems, integration errors
- **Feature Questions** - How to use specific features, best practices
- **Project Help** - Guidance on building specific types of applications
- **Integration Support** - Help with Supabase, custom domains, and other integrations

## Response Times

- **Email**: Within 24 hours (business days)
- **Discord**: Community members often respond within minutes
- **Intercom Chat**: Instant response during business hours
- **Weekend Support**: Limited availability, responses may be delayed

We're committed to helping you make the most of SteerCode. Don't hesitate to reach out!
