You can build mobile apps and websites with SteerCode for **free**.  
SteerCode is subscription-based with a **free plan** and **paid plans**.  
You use **credits** each time you send messages to SteerCode.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Free Plan" icon="gift">
    - 5 daily credits (up to 30/month)  
    - Generate apps and websites  
    - Share with friends  
    - Up to 5 public projects  
    - Community support  
    - Web and iOS app access
  </Card>

  <Card title="Premium Plan" icon="sparkles">
    - 5 daily credits (up to 30/month)  
    - 125 monthly credits (used after the free daily credits)  
    - ~10× more messages than Free  
    - Private projects  
    - Advanced integrations  
    - Publish to the web  
    - Custom domains  
    - Remove the “Built with SteerCode” badge on SteerCode websites  
    - Download the codebase
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Tip>
  When you run out of Premium credits, you can purchase **top-up credits**.
  (Currently available in the iOS app.)
</Tip>
