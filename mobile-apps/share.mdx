Share your app instantly—no installs required on iPhone. Recipients open an **App Clip** and can try your app right away, perfect for feedback and usability testing.

<video
  controls
  className="w-full aspect-video rounded-xl"
  src="/images/share-app.mp4"
></video>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Share menu">From your project, click **Share**.</Step>
  <Step title="Copy or send the link">
    Send the link to friends, teammates, or early users.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Recipients open the App Clip (iPhone)">
    On iPhone, the app opens instantly as an **App Clip**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  - **iPhone**: App Clips open without installing from the App Store. -
  **Android**: Share the preview URL; users can open it with **Expo Go**.
</Info>

<Warning>
  Shared previews are intended for testing. Do not share sensitive data,
  secrets, or private endpoints.
</Warning>

<Tip>
  Ask testers for device model, iOS/Android version, and steps to reproduce any
  issue—they’re gold for quick fixes.
</Tip>
