Launch your SteerCode creations faster by adding a **SteerCode widget** to your iOS Home Screen.

<video
  controls
  className="w-full aspect-video rounded-xl"
  src="/images/widget-app.mp4"
></video>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter jiggle mode">
    Touch and hold anywhere on your iOS Home Screen until apps jiggle, then tap
    **+** (top-left).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add the SteerCode widget">
    Search for **SteerCode**, choose a widget size, and tap **Add Widget**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick the project to pin">
    Tap the widget to **configure** it and select the project you want to open
    quickly.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Check>
  The widget launches your selected project so you can run and iterate faster.
</Check>
